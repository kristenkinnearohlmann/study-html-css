How to make a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

  1. Get 2 slices of bread
  2. Get the jar of peanut butter
  3. Get the jar of jelly
  4. Get a knife
  5. Place the slices of the bread side by side face
  6. Open the jars of peanut butter and jelly
  7. Get some peanut butter on the knife and spread on one piece of bread
  8. Get more peanut butter on the knife and spread on the other piece of bread
  9. Get some jelly on the knife and spread on top of the peanut butter on one piece of bread
  10. Get more jelly on the knife and spread on the other piece of bread
  11. Put down the knife and place the sides of the bread that have filling together
